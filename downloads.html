Supporting Programs

MFM uses or interfaces with a series of other programs. So, if you want to use the full functionality, you should install the below shown programs. All of them can be download and used for free by following the respective links above or by clicking on the according boxes below.

  • TeraCopy to copy or move the actual files.
  • Firefox to browse the web of information, torrent downloads, or read the manuals on-line.
  • FileBot to fix the names of movie or series.
  • GOM or VLC to check out the actual quality or content of the video.
  • Tixati or uTorrent to download the video.
  • MediaInfo to show detailed media information of the video.
  • Windows Explorer to drag and drop folders into and from MFM or move files to the recycle bin.
